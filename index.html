My Autobiography

Made by Alex Leal

𝔸𝕦𝕥𝕠𝕓𝕚𝕠𝕘𝕣𝕒𝕡𝕙𝕪

Im Alexis Avery Leal from Darangan Binangonan Rizal I was born on February 14 2007 in Cardona Rizal and I'm 17 years old. I have 3 siblings and I am the youngest among them all. My hobbies are playing guitar, online games, and playing badminton. Me and my family loves traveling and this is how we create memories and spend quality time. Me and my brother started traveling when I was only six years old and my first country was Hong Kong at first I was scared because it was only us two traveling and sometimes we got lost around but we managed and got the hang of it. We made friends there too with some fellow Pinoys who are working there they helped us familiarize the place around our hotel and showed us where the transportation is located. When we got home in the Philippines, I told my family about our story of how we got lost, had fun, and made friends. That was the time I fell in love with traveling and airplanes.
